REQUIREMENTS
  • ✅ Financial Assistance Application Form (available at the Registrar’s Office)
  • ✅ Certification from Class Adviser that student has a grade of 85% and above last school year
  • ✅ Copy of Voter’s ID/updated voter’s certification of parents/legal guardian
  • ✅ Certification from the Punong Barangay that the applicant belongs to a poor or indigent family
  • ✅ Certificate of No Income Tax Return of parents or legal guardian; and
  • ✅ Letter of intent from the applicant attested by the parents/legal guardian
  • ✅ Sketch of the home address for verification purposes (Reference point from ValMaSci
  • ✅ Certificate of Indigency (c/o CSWD)
APPLICATION PROCEDURE
  1. ✅ Accomplish and submit to the Registrar's Office the Financial Assistance Application Form together with the following requirements on September 18 to September 29, 2023:
    • Certification from Class Adviser that student has a grade of 85% and above last school year;
    • Copy of Voter's ID of parents/ legal guardian;
    • Certification from the Punong Barangay that the applicant belongs to a poor or indigent family;
    • Certificate of No Income Tax Return of parents or legal guardian; and
    • Letter of intent from the applicant attested by the parent/legal guardian
  2. ✅ The personnel will check and verify the completeness of the requirements submitted;
  3. ✅ The Screening Committee shall determine the eligibility of the student applicant; and
  4. ✅ The student who shall qualify for financial grant will be notified in writing by the Screening Committee.
IMPORTANT DATES TO REMEMBER

    Period of Application: September 18 - 29, 2023
    CSWD Validation and Background Check: October 2 - 13, 2023
    Posting of Results: October 18, 2023
